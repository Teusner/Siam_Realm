    Welcome to SiamRealm’s documentation!¶

    Creating an animal.

    This creates an animal with a position and a direction.

    Args
    param x

    is the abscissa of the animal,

    type x

    int,

    param y

    is the ordinate of the animal,

    type y

    int,

    param dir

    is the direction of the animal,

    type dir

    numpy.array,

    param species

    is the species of the animal. It can take only the values : “Elephant” or “Rhinoceros”,

    type car

    car.

    Example
    >>> a = Animal(0, 1, np.array([0,1]), "Elephant")
